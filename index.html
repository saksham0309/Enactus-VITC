We are a nonprofit organisation
and work worldwide

ENACTUS is an international non-profit organization dedicated to inspiring students to improve the world through entrepreneurial action. A community of student, academic and business leaders committed to using the power of entrepreneurial action to transform lives and shape a better, more sustainable world.

ENtrepreneurial: initiating business ideas and solutions with passion and innovation.
ACTion: The experience of sustainable impact, activated with integrity.
US: Student, academic and business leaders collaborating to create a better world. Enactus is spread across 36 countries and partnered with over 1700 Universities empowering 1,950,000+ people

Our Projects

Nov, 2019
Project Naari

Project Naari has its roots from the very essence of the taboos, myths, and menstrual illiteracy that still continue to exist in the India of the 21st century. Since 2018, NAARI continues to raise awareness about menstruation and its basic hygiene, by educating women about their body and their basic menstrual needs.

March, 2021
Project Pattra

Pattra aims to recycle old, torn, tattered, and used paper to produce aesthetic handmade paper. The paper is intricately made and designed by the transgender community residing in Chennai.

Read More >
Jan, 2020
Project Inara

Project Inara aims at producing biodegradable bowls, candles, and accessories out of discarded coconut shells. Inara believes in “Crafting future” by training the Irular community in shell craft and generating livelihood to “Remould” their lives.

Read More >
Jun, 2019
Project Malar

Primarily, the aim is to tackle the pollution caused by flowers at its roots and hence trying to minimise the water pollution caused indirectly due to this, and provides a livelihood mainly to female scavengers by employing them right from the collection of flowers to the manufacturing of the incense sticks.

Read More >
Jan, 2020
Project Pooranya

Project Pooranya aims to metamorphose doomed fabric into beautifully upcycled products.
With the able workforce from Teynampet and Kolathur, Pooranya makes sustainable fashion
more affordable than ever.
