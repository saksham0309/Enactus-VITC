Project Inara

Project Inara was started an year ago with the aim of producing eco-friendly sustainable products made out of coconut shells in order to reduce wastage. With time Inara has expanded in its range of products and now is known for all sorts of everyday items that can be manufactured using coconut shells such as lanterns, scented candles, pendants, and bracelets!

In addition to this, all products of Inara are manufactured by a group of underprivileged women who earn a living by selling these products. Thus the objective of our PROJECT INARA is not only to generate revenue but also to upskill and employ people from marginalized sections of the society for a common cause, i.e. to attain SUSTAINABILITY.
