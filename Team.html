Enactus VITC

We at Enactus VITC believe in the power of the community and understand that together no hurdle is too tough to get over. Our mission to make lives better is driven by our commitment to the promise that Enactus portrays to the world. Every individual has power and strength, which awaits to be utilized in the correct way. To bring about a positive impact in the society, the society itself needs to pool in its resources. And we exist to make that happen.

Enactus VITC works as a mediator for those sections of the society who are underprivileged and provide them with sustainable means of livelihoods to earn their own living. To achieve our goal, we have also partnered with other associations devoted towards similar causes. The world is rapidly changing but the needs of a common man remain the same. And our efforts remain directed towards fulfilling those needs.

Our projects focus not only on exponential business endeavors but also on providing employment opportunities to vulnerable groups of people and help to supply them with any and every essentialities possible. A journey which began in the month of August in 2015, with just 10 members in count, has now expanded to become a strong space of 100 members who function single-mindedly, all directed to the goal of transforming lives of everyone who's in need, spreading sustainability and expanding the wide horizons of hope and compassion.
